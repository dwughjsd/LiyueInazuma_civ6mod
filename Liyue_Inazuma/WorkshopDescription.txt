[h1]刻晴领导的璃月和神里绫华领导的稻妻：先睹为快[/h1]

补全合集——本文明包包含了璃月和稻妻两个文明，使得在一局游戏中出现《原神》游戏中已发布的的四个国家成为可能。

[b]璃月[/b]的特色区域是[b]璃月港[/b]，它可以令灯塔在有市场的城市也提供贸易路线容量，还可以使商人变得更加廉价。特色单位[b]千岩军[/b]取代剑客，虽然更晚解锁，但却更加强大。千岩军在丘陵地区有更快的移动力。获得双倍大将军加成。

文明特性是[b]“岩与契约之港”[/b]。游戏初始便解锁“采矿业”科技。研究“数学”科技后，您发起的贸易路线目的地每有一座矿山为璃月提供生产力，到达您的城市的贸易路线目的地每有一座采石场为璃月提供金币。

[b]刻晴[/b]的特色能力是[b]“霆霓快雨”[/b]。单位购买后可立即移动。每回合建造者和军事工程师在建造后恢复全部移动力一次。善用这项能力，便可使其发展如雷霆般迅猛。

[b]稻妻[/b]的特色区域是[b]评定所[/b]，取代市政广场。评定所可奖励3个总督头衔，为拥有总督或靠近评定所的城市提供住房以及每个公民产出，但其它城市将受到非食物产出惩罚。特色单位[b]幕府军足轻[/b]拥有3点移动力。为邻接的非足轻陆地军事单位 +5战斗力。若使用[b]神里绫华[/b]领导稻妻，他们还可以建设[b]社奉行[/b]，使拥有总督或靠近评定所的城市从每座特色区域中获得文化和宜居度。

稻妻的文明特性是[b]“宁寂永恒之国”[/b]。每座欣喜若狂的城市都能提升所有城市的科技值和文化值产出，但积累双倍厌战情绪。[b]神里绫华[/b]的特色能力[b]“白鹭公主”[/b]可以帮助稻妻获得更多欣喜若狂的城市。他们可以加速建设娱乐中心和水上乐园区域和建筑。这些区域和建筑还能提升所在城市的伟人点数产出。

您要领导追求繁荣的璃月还是心向永恒的稻妻？您将会如何带领他们建立起经得起时间考验的文明？

[h1]模组特性：[/h1]

[b]强力而不超模。[/b]每位领袖的能力均鼓励一种独特的游玩方式，并在设计时充分考虑了领袖的能力强度，使其拥有充分的竞争力但并不显著过强。

[h1]Keqing Leads Liyue and Kamisato Ayaka Leads Inazuma: First Look[/h1]

Complete the set - this civilization pack contains Liyue and Inazuma and makes it possible for you to have a Civilization game with all 4 nations released in the game Genshin Impact present.

The unique district of [b]Liyue[/b] is [b]Liyue Harbor[/b]. It allows the Lighthouse in it grants Trade Route capacity even if the city has a Market. Their unique unit is [b]Millelith[/b] which replaces Swordsman. Though unlocked later, it is a stronger unit. Millelith has faster Movement on Hill terrain, and receives double Great General bonus.

Liyue's unique ability is [b]Harbor of Stone and Contracts[/b]. They begin the game with the Mining technology unlocked. Your outgoing Trade Routes grant Production to Liyue per Mine at the destination. Trade Routes sent to your cities grant Gold to Liyue per Quarry at the destination.

[b]Keqing[/b]'s leader ability is [b]Driving Thunder[/b]. Units can immediately move after purchase. Builders and Military Engineers have their Movement fully restored after taking a building action once per turn. Fully utilize her ability, and they can develop blazingly fast.

The unique district of Inazuma is [b]Triarchal Council[/b] which replaces the Government Plaza district. It awards +3 Governor Titles instead of 1. The Triarchal Council district and each government building built in the district provide Housing and all yields per Citizen to your cities with Governors or near it, but other cities receive non-Food yields penalty. Inazuman unique unit [b]Shogunate Ashigaru[/b] has 3 Movement and grants +5 Combat Strength to adjacent military units other than Shogunate Ashigaru. If Inazuma is led by [b]Kamisato Ayaka[/b], they can build [b]Yashiro Commission[/b] which makes cities with Governors or near it receives bonus Culture and Amenities for each specialty district it construct.

The unique ability of [b]Inazuma[/b] is [b]Realm of Tranquil Eternity[/b]. All cities receive Science and Culture for each Ecstatic city， but they accumulate double war weariness. The leader ability [b]Shirasagi Himegimi[/b] of [b]Kamisato Ayaka[/b] helps Inazuma to have more Ecstatic cities. They build Entertainment Complex and Water Park districts and buildings faster, and these districts and buildings boost Great People points output of the city they are in.

Which one do you want to lead, Liyue pursuing prosperity, or Inazuma eagering for eternity? How would you lead them to build a civilization that stand the test of time?

[h1]Mod Highlights:[/h1]

[b]"Powered" without "Over"[/b]: Each leader has several abilities that promote a unique play-style. This mod is designed to make abilities of all leaders included competent but not overpowered.

[b]Lore-Loyalty[/b]: This mod is made with a fine research of the original game, and provides full lists of named places, citizen names, city names and diplomacy statements to better represent lore and leader characteristics.

[b]Harmonized Mix[/b]: All art assets included in this mod are processed to fit the need of Civilization VI, so that they can display correctly and neatly.

[b]Background Music and Leader Speeches[/b]: All Liyue and Inazuman leaders have their own music and leader speech audio.

[h1]Known issues:[/h1]

Music playback can be sometimes glitchy, with the same issues of some official civilizations like Vietnam and Portugal.

Some Civilopedia articles are in Simplified Chinese only.
